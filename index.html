Who We Are

Beyond the
Spectacle

We are a collective of activists, artists, organisers, and documentarians committed to cultural resistance. We believe that memory is political, images are evidence, and every protest is a performance.

Founded in response to the erasure of public space and the criminalisation of dissent, we archive what institutions would rather forget, and amplify voices that power tries to silence.

Our work spans street photography, zine publishing, live events, and community organising — always rooted in solidarity, never in spectacle.
